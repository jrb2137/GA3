Product Description
Interactive notebooks are a great way to make learning fun and provide students a reference place for the skills they have learned.

If you teach second grade phonics skills this is a MUST have resource!

The following skills are included in this resource:
beginning/ending sh
beginning/ending th
beginning/Ending ch
l blends with short vowels
r blends with short vowels
s blends with short vowels
final blends
ending ch, -tch
3 letter blends
oa
ow
ai
ay
ee
ea
igh
y sounds like i
y sounds like e
ie
ew
ue
ui
2 sounds of oo
oi/oy
ou/ow
ai word families
ea word families
ee word families
a_e word families (silent e)
i_e word families (silent e)
o_e word families (silent e)
contractions (not, have, will, is)
ph
r controlled patterns (ar, are, air, er, eer, ear, ir, ire, or, our, ore, ur)
compound words
hard c/soft c
hard g/soft g
silent letters- mb, wr, gh, kn
3 sounds of -ed
suffix -ing
change y to i before adding er
suffix -er and -est
2 syllable word
3 syllable words
homophones
plurals (add s, add es, change y to i, change f to v, irregular)
Prefixes re, mis, pre, un, dis

All materials are in black and white, so they are very printer friendly.
